About Me

Hi there! I'm Becky, a highly skilled and passionate UX designer and video editor. With my extensive design and video production background, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project I undertake. My ultimate goal is to create captivating digital experiences that tell compelling stories through design, and I'm confident in my ability to do so. On my portfolio, you'll find a handpicked selection of my work that showcases my skills, creativity, and dedication to delivering exceptional results. I'm always eager to collaborate on exciting new projects, so please don't hesitate to reach out!
